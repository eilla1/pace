Breathing Exercises

Practice mindfulness by following along with guided breathing exercises.





Walking Meditation

1. Stand up straight with your back upright but not stiff. Feel your feet touching the ground and let your weight distribute evenly.

2. Curl the thumb of your left hand in and wrap your fingers around it. Place it just above your belly button. Wrap your right hand around your left hand.

3. Drop your gaze slightly. This helps you maintain focus.

4. Step out with your left foot. Feel it swing, feel the heel hit the ground, now the ball, now the toes. Feel the same as the right foot comes forward.

5. Walk at a steady pace. When your attention wanders, bring it back to the sensations of your feet touching the ground.

Journaling

Choose a prompt from the list and respond to it in the text box below. The goal of this exercise is to bring promote creativity through mindful, diligent concentration.

  • Letters from the future: What would you tell your January 1, 2020 self?
  • Write down all your wildest dreams for 2021.
  • What’s one quality that you’ve learned to appreciate about yourself?
  • What’s one new hobby, habit, or routine you’re glad that you picked up?
  • Write down at least three goals you would like to achieve by the end of the year.
  • I feel most peaceful when… I feel most angry when…
  • What unhealthy habits do you need to cut out?
  • How do you find fulfillment?
  • What is a challenge that you faced today? How did you solve it?
  • Write 5 things that make you smile.
